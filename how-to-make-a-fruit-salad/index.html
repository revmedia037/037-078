How to Make a Fruit Salad

Making a fruit salad is a refreshing way to enjoy seasonal fruits. Start by selecting a variety of ripe fruits such as berries, melons, citrus, and tropical fruits. Wash and chop the fruits into bite-sized pieces and place them in a large bowl. Add a squeeze of fresh lemon or lime juice to prevent browning and enhance the flavors. Optionally, drizzle with honey or a light fruit syrup for added sweetness. Toss gently to combine and refrigerate until ready to serve.
