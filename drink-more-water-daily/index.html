Drink More Water Daily

Proper hydration impacts every system in your body positively. Start your day with a glass of water to rehydrate after sleep. Carry a reusable water bottle as a visual reminder to drink. Infuse water with fruits or herbs if you dislike plain water. Set hourly reminders if you frequently forget to hydrate. Notice how your skin, energy, and focus improve with better hydration. Drink before meals to aid digestion and prevent overeating. Monitor your urine color as a simple hydration gauge. Proper water intake is one of the easiest ways to boost overall health.
